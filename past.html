Space Dodge

It is 2014 and you are dismayed that the predicted Mayan apocalypse has yet to have wiped out the entirety of the human race and you have fled Earth in the hopes that your repetitive premonitions have been correct. You have stolen a classified space shuttle from the NASA headquarters in Houston, Texas and are travelling at 5,000 MPH towards an unknown destination. Standing between you and your hallucinogenic dream of a far-distant Utopian society there are obstacles in your way... Most of the game and website were made by me.

Rainy Birds

Identifying a gap in the games market, two friends and I attempted to take advantage of the lack of 'Flappy Birds' game for the Windows Phone platform. We quickly made a clone of the game, which was entirely free and Public Domain. This game quickly gained attention and recieved over 150,000 downloads in the first week.

Rhino Rampage

This is a project that I am currently doing for my Creative Media GCSE in school. The concept of the game is you are a fierce rhino who gets abducted from your natural jungle habitat by poachers. You are then forced to escape from captivity, smashing anything in your way, to make your way back home.
